AI Class Project

Artificial Intelligence Course | Fall 2024

This project builds an automated, regime-based portfolio allocation system. It ingests 13 macro indicators, normalizes them, scores probable market regimes, and generates allocation recommendations with full risk analytics.

The work blends economic intuition with programmatic pipelines—producing both investment-grade memos and testing harnesses that surfaced logic gaps and improved reliability of model outputs.

Project Overview

Objective: Turn noisy macro data into confident, explainable portfolio shifts for varied market regimes.

What it does: Normalizes macro indicators (breakeven inflation, financial conditions, etc.), calculates regime probabilities, then computes returns, volatility, Sharpe/Sortino, drawdowns, and VaR across asset classes to propose allocations.

Why it matters: Produces transparent allocation decisions and investor-friendly summaries instead of black-box signals.
