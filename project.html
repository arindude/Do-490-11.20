AI Class Project

Artificial Intelligence Course | Fall 2024

This project analyzes the U.S. real estate industry and how GenAI is reshaping it. I use real data to size and segment the industry, then evaluate AI’s impact on brokerage, property management, valuation, and day-to-day workflows.

I’m creating original charts for key trends, writing a concise industry report, and mapping how my skills align with an AI-driven future in real estate.

Project Overview

Objective: Describe the size, structure, and maturity of U.S. real estate—and show where GenAI is already changing the industry.

What it does: Combines market data, segmentation, and process maps to highlight AI-driven shifts in brokerage, property management, valuation, and workflows.

Why it matters: Helps stakeholders understand real adoption paths, efficiency gains, and evolving roles—plus how my own skill set fits into the transition.
