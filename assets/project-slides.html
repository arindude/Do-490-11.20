AI Class Project — Presentation Deck

Automated Regime-Based Portfolio Allocation System

This short deck-style page distills the presentation content: motivation, methodology, signals, allocations, and investor takeaway.

Slide 1

Problem & Objective

Bridge macro noise and portfolio action by mapping indicators to regimes, then producing transparent allocations with guardrails.

Slide 2

Macro Inputs

13 signals including breakeven inflation, financial conditions, growth, and risk appetite. Z-score normalization and smoothing to stabilize signals.

Slide 3

Regime Logic

Probability engine classifies regimes (risk-on / balanced / defensive) and sets allocation bands plus exposure caps.

Slide 4

Analytics

Computes returns, volatility, Sharpe/Sortino, drawdowns, and VaR for candidate portfolios; ranks by risk-adjusted performance.

Slide 5

Outputs & Narrative

Generates allocation tables plus a concise investor memo summarizing the regime call, rationale, and risk notes.

Slide 6

Reliability & QA

Controlled test cases exposed model edge cases (pricing/logic); fixes reduced failure rates by >90%.
